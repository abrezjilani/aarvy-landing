About Us

Aarvy Healthcare Super Speciality Hospital was founded by Dr. Vikram Singh and Dr. Renu Yadav as an industry-transforming initiative. It is the first state-of-the-art quaternary hospital in New Gurugram. Aarvy Healthcare is structured on contemporary and environment-friendly technology.

The hospital caters to

the requirements of the provision of emergent healthcare. The facility offers multiple sharing, twin sharing, single, deluxe, and suite options for patients.

It also offers an

optimum mix of emergency, department-specific intensive care, and department-specific daycare beds, keeping in view the treatment requirements and modalities.
